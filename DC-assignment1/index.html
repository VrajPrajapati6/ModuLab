Core Modulation Types

A

ASK

Amplitude Shift Keying: The amplitude of the carrier wave is varied to represent binary data. High amplitude for 1, low/zero for 0.

F

FSK

Frequency Shift Keying: The frequency of the carrier is shifted between two values to represent 0s and 1s. Reliable for noisy channels.

P

PSK

Phase Shift Keying: The phase of the carrier wave is shifted (usually 180°) to represent data changes. Most efficient & reliable.
